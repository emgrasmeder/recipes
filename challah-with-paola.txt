#### Mix together:
206g water
1 packages dry active yeast (
1.5 teaspoons of honey

#### Then add:
56 grams butter/oil
2 eggs, 1 at a time
85g honey
1.5 teaspoons salt

make a bowl out of 450g flour and add mixture slowly to flour

---
Knead until smooth
put in a greased bowl
let rise in warm place for 1 hour
punch and wait for another half hour
braid the challah 
brush one egg on the challah knots
let rise in refigerator for 1 hour

preheat over to 290C
egg wash again
bake 35-40? minutes, or until golden
